# Automotive Safety Front Bumper Design and Analysis

## Objective
To design and analyze an automotive safety front bumper capable of improving impact resistance and maintaining structural integrity under loading conditions.

## Tools Used

- SolidWorks
- ANSYS

## Methodology

- Designed the front bumper geometry in SolidWorks
- Applied material properties
- Generated mesh in ANSYS
- Applied loading and boundary conditions
- Performed structural analysis
- Evaluated stress and deformation behavior


## Conclusion

The design was evaluated for structural performance and impact resistance to determine its suitability for automotive safety applications.
